WOMEN IN STEM ADVOCACY

As a Creative Director at Reinvented Magazine I have been leading my team to create branding designs focused on female empowerment in stem.
I am also leading the chrome extension development project for them.

As the President of the nonprofit organization GAITEway (Girls in AI and Tech Education) I have been leading AI workshops for female middle school students in underrepresented communities to introduce them to AI.

As the founder of a VEX robotics club and lead engineer for team 2772J I have been leading an apprenticeship program for girls interested in robotics. I've been teaching about the mechanical, software, design, and documentation aspects of robotics.
